Welcome to Sable Safari

Where fun and nature dance together

If you're looking to experience vast landscapes, unforgettable wildlife encounters and breathtaking sunsets, then Sable Safari is the perfect getaway for you! Sable Safari is a place where you can see wildlife upclose, get your adrenaline pumping with a bungee-jump and finish the day off with a cool beverage and a relaxing sunset cruise. Perfectly nest about 10 minutes from the infamous landmark, Victoria Falls, Sable Safari is here to give you an exhilirating holiday right in the hands of mother nature herself!
