Where fun and nature dance together

If you're looking to experience vast landscapes, unforgettable wildlife encounters and breathtaking sunsets, then Sable Safari is the perfect getaway for you! Sable Safari is a place a place where you can see wildlife upclose, get your adrenaline pumping with a bungee-jump and finish the day off with a beer and a relaxing sunset cruise.
