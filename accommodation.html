Where you can stay at Sable Safari

Here at Sable Safari, we offer rooms for all of your needs! Take a look at our three choice of accommodation...

Luxury Suites

If you're looking to treat yourself to a luxury getaway, with beautiful views in every direction, 24/7 room-service and access to all of our facilities on-site, then book one of our luxury suites!

Tented Campsites

For the budget-friendly and nature-loving folk, we offer tented Campsites where you can stay in a ready-made tent filled with your basic needs: a comfy bed, fire-lantern and mosquito-nets to keep you safe from those pesky bugs!

Safari Lodges

Our Safari Lodges give you a chance to experience the best of both worlds! Some luxury facilities with an authentic African experience in the wild bush. Our lodges are located right at the heart of the Safari, so you can experience close encounters with wildlife from your doorstep!
