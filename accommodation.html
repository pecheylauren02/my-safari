Safari Lodges

Nyati Lodge

Nyati Lodge is one of our beautiful, spacious and cozy lodges with an excellent view of the surroundings. It has a more authentic Safari feel to it, and has plenty of space for your entire family! Take a look!

$60/night

Nzou Lodge

Nzou Lodge is one of our cozy wooden lodges, with two twin-beds. It is located right at the heart of the Safari, so you can experience animal encounters right at your doorstep. Can't get better than that!

$50/night

Ingwe Lodge

Ingwe Lodge is almost identical to Nzou Lodge, although it has a queen bed which is perfect for couples looking to experience wildlife at its best. It is also located at the heart of the Safari, so you can see animals up close!

$50/night

Tented Campsites

Campsite One

For the budget-friendly and nature-loving folk, we offer Campsites where you can stay in a ready-made tent filled with your basic needs: a comfy bed, fire-lantern and mosquito-nets to keep you safe from those pesky bugs!

$20/night

Campsite Two

This Campsite will be just as fun, because you'll be bringing your own tent and camping gear with you. Remember to bring warm clothes, mosquito repellent and a small gas stove to cook your food. Have fun wildin' it out!

$10/night

Campsite Three

Campsite Three is very similar to Campsite One. We offer all of the same facilities and furniture for your tent. It's just in a different location on the Safari. So you might see different wildlife to the visitors of Campsite One.

$15/night
